Did you know? Saarbrücken hosted the TaCoS also in 1995, 2006 and 2013.

Saarbrücken

    Saarbrücken is the cultural and political capital of the Saarland and located at the French border.

    Regarding accomodation, we will organize a coli couch surfing. Apart from that, there is also a youth hostel, which unfortunately is fully booked on Saturday. If your budget is small and you couldn't find a couch, you could book the youth hostel on Friday and Sunday and move in between.

Saarland University

    The main campus of Saarland University is located outside of town on a hill in the forest.

    The computational linguistics department, where the TaCoS will take place, can be found right here in the buildings C7.1 - C7.4.

    The campus also consists of other non-university research institutes, many of which specialize in computer science. In particular, there are the DFKI (Deutsches Forschungszentrum für Künstliche Intelligenz) and two Max Planck institutes.
